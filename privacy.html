Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

OpenWith is a macOS utility that helps you open Finder directories in your favorite applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

OpenWith does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No network requests
  • No crash reporting
  • No user accounts

Data Storage

All app configuration (your list of configured applications and preferences) is stored locally on your device using App Group UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

OpenWith does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data.

Finder Extension

The OpenWith Finder Extension accesses the current directory path in Finder solely to open it in your chosen application. This information is not stored or transmitted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
